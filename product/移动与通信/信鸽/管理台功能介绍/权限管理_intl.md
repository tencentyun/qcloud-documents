## Role Category & Overview

### Admin
**Who:** App creator, project leader, team leader, developer, etc.
**Overview:** With complete platform permissions, the admin can delete Apps, add/delete/edit other roles, or delete other administrators.

### Operator
**Who:** Platform user, personnel responsible for pushing messages
**Overview:** This role can push messages and view data, but cannot delete Apps, add/edit/delete roles, or view ACCESS KEY/SECRET KEY.

### Viewer
**Who:** People who view the platform data, such as interns and contractors.
**Overview:** This role can only view data, but cannot create pushes, delete Apps, or add/edit roles.
