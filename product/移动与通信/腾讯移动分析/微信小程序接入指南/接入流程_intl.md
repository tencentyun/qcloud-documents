
## Connection Procedure
1. Log in to [Tencent Mobile Analytics homepage](http://mta.qq.com/mta/ctr_index/wx), and click the banner of WeChat Mini Program or [**Use Now**](http://mta.qq.com/mta/ctr_index/wx) button on the WeChat Mini Program product page to complete the registration by following the procedure.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/a1b9ab868d5d83b37eec147897b91ff1/image.png)
2. After the registration is completed, click to view **Instructions on SDK Configuration**.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/b9993ff4b735d12f6dfc83cf632ec442/image.png)
3. Download and configure SDK according to **Instructions on SDK Configuration**, and call mta.Page.init() on Page.onLoad of the page to be reported to complete reporting.
