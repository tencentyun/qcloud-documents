1. Integrate SDK ([Download SDK](http://mta.qq.com/mta/ctr_index/download)) containing the installation source analysis.
2. Create a promotion program.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/a79104e977236fc670f66e6fb27ad632/image.png)
3. Click **Promotion Channel Configuration** to perform the corresponding configuration.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/9e613f795e6f53f27a53c711d9f067c2/image.png)
4. Configure the installation channel.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/0883aa19d049f3a942fda04f2273d0a5/image.png)
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/61b17800b7086046f4c5a8d7adf568e5/image.png)
>**Note:**
>[Promotion Channel]: Platform MTA on which you plan to deliver ads in your promotion program supports multiple landing links. You can perform the corresponding configuration based on your needs in Promotion Channel.

5. After the configuration is completed, click **View** in Tlink link to get Tlink for advertising. 
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/1d61bf3806467532d0bb1d455af55d8e/image.png)
