### Select Highly Valuable and Active Users in App:
Go to **Application Analysis** -> **User Mining** -> **User Segmentation** in the MTA console, and then click **New User Group**.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/34ec55e936c5abb354cf27fae10275c7/image.png)
Select a group of highly active users according to active criteria, such as users that has been active for 5 weeks in a row, then select the users and save as a user group.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/f8420876eee746c7f431adaf35d544a8/image.png)
Or select **Custom Event** and group users based on the number of their behaviors with the highest value. For example, as [Declare Stock] is considered as the most valuable behavior for stock-related Apps, you can filter out users that used [Declare Stock] for over 10 times via user grouping.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/143bd1862017b7c0b2027ec253ccc39e/image.png)
After the user group is saved, click to activate the user group. You may receive the calculation result of the user group on the next day. However, you can configure your WIN account without waiting for the calculation result.

### Configuring Tencent Social Ads Account
Go to **App Analysis** -> **Discover Users** -> **Export Group** on MTA console, and select **Export to Tencent Social Ads**.  
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/d5bb1e2efff3e91581ae721d991325d7/image.png)
On the exporting page, click **Configure Now** to configure the target account of Tencent Social Ads.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/4c88e59c7de077e4618b6257b054a406/image.png)
On the opened authorization page, you need to log in to the QQ account that has the access permission on DMP management platform of Tencent Social Ads and grant the authorization to MTA.

>**Notes:**
>1. If an account of Tencent Social Ads has not been activated for your business, please visit [Tencent Social Ads](http://e.qq.com/ads) to activate the advertising platform of Tencent Social Ads for account activation.
>2. If an account of Tencent Social Ads has been activated for your business, but DMP management platform of Tencent Social Ads has not been activated, please visit [Data Management Platform of Tencent Social Ads](http://de.qq.com/contact.html) and scroll to the bottom of the page for contact information.
>3. If an account of Tencent Social Ads has been activated for you company but cannot be shared with you, you can add this QQ account to the system in the configuration management, and ask your colleague to complete the authorization operation.

![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/f076470776c01d0738974bb5377494ec/image.png)
After the authorization is completed, check whether the account configuration is successful. If yes, close the page (if any user group that can be exported is available, select the user group and export it to Tencent Social Ads directly).
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/dca25fe280d87bd5b7abac7e25942278/image.png)

### Exporting, Disseminating and Advertising to the Group in DMP System
After the user group calculation is completed, click **Export** for the corresponding group in the group list, and then choose **Export to Tencent Social Ads**. On the popped-up page, configure user groups and fields to be exported, and then click **Start Exporting**.
>**Note:**
>The group dissemination effect is optimal when the number of users in the user group is greater than or equal to 10,000. 

![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/5eb8320ab08b8aeec7499a1737a3332e/image.png)
After clicking **Start Exporting**, you can click **View Task Progress**. If the group has been successfully exported to Tencent Social Ads, you will receive a notification reminder (in the reminder method configured in the last step).
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/01b32de5b1c6721abb1937e56069b546/image.png)
After receiving the notification reminder, log in to the data [platform system](http://de.qq.com/) of Tencent Social Ads to view the corresponding group status.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/1fb79242d503e10c1458fe602792661a/image.png)
If the group status is "available", select the group and then select **Operation** -> **Intelligent Extension**.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/1c443c584bf498a4e6f1c4ac77d1646e/image.png)
Select the extension level, remove the existing users, and then save the result. After the group calculation is completed, you can select this group to deliver ads in Tencent Social Ads.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/52c84fb577e3266c0645e11bcabec397/image.png)
For any questions, please contact our customer service in the lower right corner on MTA official website.
