### Preconditions
1. App integrates the latest statistics SDK of MTA (Note: V3.2.0 and later versions are new versions which support connecting by scanning QR code using WeChat, and versions earlier than V3.2.0 are old versions which support touching with four fingers and connecting by shaking);
2. Register your own App successfully on MTA console.

### Steps

Step 1: Log in to the MTA console, and enter the **Visualized Event Tracking** page;
**New version:** Scan QR code using WeChat to connect the page
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/04f0febda44701247ac9055c1d7a7fc6/image.png)
**Old version:** Touch with four fingers, and shake to connect the page
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/fd86c76441ee4eb7a750dbb7c0e71524/image.png)
Step 2: Connect a device on the console;
**New version:** Scan QR code using WeChat to connect
1. Scan QR code using WeChat to enter the page below. You are prompted to open the link using a browser.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/68ccd2ceda901427f308c8c4329c6147/image.png)
2. After the mobile browser is opened, you are redirected to the following page. Click **Start Connecting** to pull the App to be tracked.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/9823a23801f3e1a8b2c9850cb7418221/image.png)
3. After a few seconds, PC console enters the page below, which represents a successful connection.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/6de4e476f250649886b16d0fd5321500/image.png)

**Old version:** Touch with four fingers, and shake

Click **Start Connecting Device**, and the **Connecting...** screen below appears. You need to do the following:
1. Open the App to be tracked on the phone;
2. Within 10 seconds, press the screen with four slightly spread fingers (do not rush to press on the start screen);
3. Keep four fingers on the screen for at least 3 seconds.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/2469cc13bebb97188e88d5845f5fe217/image.png)
If the following screen appears during connection, it indicates that a mobile is already connected to MTA. Check whether it is your own mobile. If yes, click **Connect**. If not, click **Cancel**, and repeat the previous steps to connect.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/142ea659c7ae87e64004e441d15363e8/image.png)
>**Notes:**
>1. Make sure that your SDK version is 1.3.0 or above before starting;
>2. Real-time data generated by visualized event tracking may consume traffic. Debug when WiFi is available.
>3. If the connecting failed all the time, restart the App after exiting the process directly (required for Android and iOS), and try again;
>4. If TencentOpenApi is used in the project, update TencentOpenApi to the latest version.

Step 3: Perform tracking operations

On the App page, the red frame represents areas available for tracking, while the green one represents tracked areas. The left column contains operation logs of tracking area, and log records show when you click the tracking area. The right column contains a list of tracked areas, and related operations can be performed.
Specific tracking example:
1. Click the [red frame] in the upper left corner of the App, and name it in the popup box. Click OK, and the tracking will appear in the tracked list on the right column.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/0bb26801137fc67a60826a68c99fc384/image.png)
2. Click **Issue**, and a confirmation dialog box appears. Click OK to enter the page below, and the tracking is successfully completed.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/7ec756c3d2b1a5ce00fa84fcd4f829be/image.png)
