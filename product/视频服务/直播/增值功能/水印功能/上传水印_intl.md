To use the watermark feature, you need to upload the watermark image, and then add a watermark under **Global Settings** -> **Watermark Management** on [Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/live/livestat).
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/5efeb8d14d9289c7c7ba2c955d032a40/image.png)
You can obtain watermark ID and name after the watermark is added successfully. Create a watermark with the ID of 14049 and the name of "test".
