## Signing up for a Tencent Cloud Account

Before using Tencent Cloud Video service, you need to register a Tencent Cloud account and submit relevant information. For more information on how to register and log in, please see [How to Get Started](http://bbs.qcloud.com/forum.php?mod=viewthread&tid=2387&extra=page=1%20%E6%96%B0%E6%89%8B%E4%B8%93%E5%8C%BA) documentation:


## Activating LVB Service

You can activate LVB service in the following ways.

Select "LVB" on Tencent Cloud console as shown below:

![](http://qzonestyle.gtimg.cn/qzone/vas/opensns/res/img/yunzhiboshiyong-1.png)

### Step 1: Identity Verification

Users need to complete the identity verification before using video service. Both individual verification or enterprise verification are supported. You can directly access the "Developer Identity Verification" page by following the step below. See the figure:

![](//mccdn.qcloud.com/static/img/5072d490be8560ae6ca17279c19c8861/image.png)

You can also click "Account" -> "Account Information" -> "Business Information" in the upper right corner of the console, and then click the "Identity Verification" button to complete verification.

As shown below:

![](http://qzonestyle.gtimg.cn/qzone/vas/opensns/res/img/zhiborenzheng.png)

### Step 2: Submit business information

After passing the verification, you need to submit business information, including App information such as user type, enterprise name and business information.

Now, the LVB service employs approval mechanism. The final approval result is sent to you via SMS message or email within 5 work days, including a conclusion indicating whether you get the approval.

## Activating LVB Service

You can activate LVB service in the following ways.

# Renewal
After the service becomes in arrears, you can directly click "Buy Now" button at the lower left corner of the console. You can enable renewal in real time by selecting prepaid traffic package.

# Upgrade
To upgrade traffic package, you can directly click "Buy Now" button at the lower left corner of the console. You can enable renewal in real time by selecting prepaid traffic package.

# Arrears
LVB service is terminated in real time when it becomes in arrears. An message indicating that you have arrears is shown on the status interface of the console.
In this case, you can still create channels and manage existing channels. However, the downstream LVB service is terminated. You are recommended to monitor the current traffic status in real time and ensure that the traffic is available.
