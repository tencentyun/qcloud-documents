LVB software is applicable to various scenarios with the options of other products of Tencent Cloud video solution to implement a diversity of scenarios including the game LVB, video portal, and social LVB.
For more information, please see [solution page](http://cloud.tencent.com/solution/video.html)

## Game LVB

#### Main Features

It meets the LVB requirements for standard large concurrency and low latency.
By using LVB publishing platform with more than 500 nodes, it helps video portal users possess the same publishing capacities as the underlying service level of Tencent Video, while completely and rapidly covering users around the world.
The system can be easily connected with professional video recording equipment, broadcast control platform, and any other standard HLS/RTMP LVB sources. Anything can be quickly published, no matter it is a popular game VJ or a competitive event.
It also provides a flexible player to help quickly cover players in all dimensions through mobile Apps, web pages and a variety of display modes.
Detailed statistical analysis can help game platforms accurately grasp the behavioral characteristics of players.
Scenarios can be recorded in real time and interfaced with VOD platform, providing comprehensive video recording and replay services.
The IM SDK feature can be integrated into mobile and web Apps, providing text-based and picture-based instant messaging capabilities to enrich the LVB interactive scenarios.

## Video Portal


#### Main Features

By using LVB publishing platform with more than 500 nodes, it helps video portal users possess the same publishing capacities as the underlying service level of Tencent Video.
The system can be easily connected with professional video recording equipment, broadcast control platform, and any other standard HLS/RTMP LVB sources, as well as quickly publish various LVB contents such as concerts, variety shows, sport events, corporate activities and so on.
It also provides a flexible player to help video portals easily reach their end users through mobile Apps, web pages and a variety of display modes.
Detailed statistical analysis can help service providers accurately grasp the behavioral characteristics of users. Scenarios can be directly recorded and interfaced with VOD platform, providing comprehensive video services.
