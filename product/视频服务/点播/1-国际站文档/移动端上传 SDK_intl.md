## Overview

Currently, Tencent Cloud VOD provides Android SDK and iOS SDK for mobile devices.

Apart from APIs used to upload videos, mobile SDKs also provide a rich collection of video editing APIs (for example, video clipping, stitching, filtering, subtitling, etc.) to satisfy customer demands.
