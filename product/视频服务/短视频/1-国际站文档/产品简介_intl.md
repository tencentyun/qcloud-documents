## Solution Overview

In recent years, short video industry grows very rapidly and has become a new trend following LVB, with creative play and fresh video content. To help developers quickly create and release short video Apps, Tencent Cloud VOD launched a one-stop short video solution that covers video creation, upload, processing, distribution, playback and other processes.

Short video (UserGeneratedShortVideo) service: We provide capturing, splicing, special effects, sharing, playback, and other client components to make it easy for developers to focus on the business and develop mobile-based shot video business in combination with powerful cloud capabilities like fast uploading, transcoding, storage, and Tencent data like IM, social networking, user profiles.

This document is intended to help customers experience, activate, purchase, and use Tencent Cloud short video service in the fastest and easiest way.
