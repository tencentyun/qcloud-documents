## SDK Feature List

<table>
   <tr style="background:#cccccc">
      <td font-weight:bold>Feature module</td>
      <td font-weight:bold>Feature point</td>
      <td font-weight:bold>Description</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td rowspan="2">Shooting</td>
      <td>Focusing</td>
      <td>Manual focusing during short video shooting</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>Shooting control</td>
      <td>Control for start/stop of shooting, timeline, and lighting</td>
   </tr>

   <tr>
      <td rowspan="4">Clipping</td>
      <td>Frame timeline</td>
      <td>Display key frames at an interval set on the timeline during clipping</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>Preview by frame</td>
      <td>Display in the preview window the frame where the cursor is placed when you move the cursor on the timeline</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>Clipping cursor</td>
      <td>Move two cursors on the timeline to mark the beginning of the fragment you want to keep</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>Speed setting</td>
      <td>Set the speed of the short video, such as n times the normal speed</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td rowspan="3">Mix</td>
      <td>Selecting an audio file</td>
      <td>Select a built-in sound file or an mp3 file from your mobile as the background music</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>Clipping an audio</td>
      <td>After you select an audio file, clip an appropriate length to play in the video</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>Setting the sound volume</td>
      <td>Set the background music volume (subject to the video sound)</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td rowspan="4">Special effects</td>
      <td>Beauty filter parameter settings</td>
      <td>Set the level of whitening, dermabrasion, sharpness, face slimming and eye enlarging when shooting</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>Adding a filter</td>
      <td>Add lighting effect when shooting and editing a video</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>Green screen keying-out</td>
      <td>Replace green elements (such as pure green background) with others (such as dynamic background/PPT) during shooting</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>Adding special face effects</td>
      <td>Add effects such as facial contortion or stickers on the recognized faces when shooting and editing</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td rowspan="4">Subtitle</td>
      <td>Selecting a subtitle container</td>
      <td>When adding a subtitle, you can select a style for the border and background of a subtitle, such as a bubble one.</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>Setting the subtitle position</td>
      <td>Set the position of the subtitle displayed in the screen by dragging and dropping</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>Text setting</td>
      <td>Enter text in the container, and change the color and size of the text based on the container's style and size</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>Setting the start frame to display the subtitle</td>
      <td>You can add multiple subtitles for a short video and set a starting point for each subtitle</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td rowspan="3">Splicing</td>
      <td>Selecting files</td>
      <td>Select video files from the mobile on the client</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>Order adjustment</td>
      <td>Adjust the order of the selected files</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>File splicing</td>
      <td>Splice the selected video files in a predetermined order to create a new video</td>
   </tr>
</table>
