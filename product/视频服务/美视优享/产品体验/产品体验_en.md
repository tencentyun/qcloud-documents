## Experiencing Intelligent Porn Detection
Developers can try Porn Detection out on [Try-out Platform](http://cloud.tencent.com/event/pd). Account registration is not required on the platform.
1. Click "Add Picture", and select the picture to be detected in the pop-up window.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/9cfc6f25d3cf082029bbeb2a8b5f61ed/image.png)
2. After the picture is uploaded, click "Start detection".
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/4c8349cb0dc410291137854d3b67f494/image.png)
3. View the detection result.
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/44576b0b092fd36a096d43a73ed14e35/image.png)
