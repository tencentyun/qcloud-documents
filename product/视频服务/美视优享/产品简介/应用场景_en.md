## LVB
Manual porn detection is costly in ILVB scenarios as the LVB is hosted concurrently in tens of thousands of rooms. Porn monitoring in LVB is done by means of machine recognition and highlighted warning on a 24/7 basis to ensure a high coverage and a healthy platform. Suspicious rooms can be detected rapidly from massive room screenshots to guarantee response in seconds. Therefore, a second-level review can be done with fewer manual interventions to take further actions such as sending warnings, blocking stream and relevant accounts, etc.
Beauty Effect is a dynamic image processing component built on Tencent YouTu's excellent face recognition and tracking technology. The component analyzes VJ's face area in real-time, and applies well-designed stickers to appropriate positions. With dozens of stickers in our library, your LVB product can be given more interactivity and lots of fun.
