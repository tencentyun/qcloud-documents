### Download Public and Private Keys

Open the App list, and then click **App Configuration** for the relevant App.

![](//avc.qcloud.com/wiki2.0/im/imgs/20151123112657_31405.png)

Click **Download Public and Private Keys** under **Account System Integration** to obtain the public and private keys generated by the system.

![](//avc.qcloud.com/wiki2.0/im/imgs/20151123112739_69490.png)

### Set App Admin

Only after the self-owned account is integrated can an admin be set. Click here to open the App list. If the relevant App is not available yet, please apply for activating it in the page displayed.

![](//avc.qcloud.com/wiki2.0/im/imgs/20151016031051_80560.png)

Click the relevant App Configuration, go to the area as shown below, and then enter the admin account. A maximum of three admin accounts are allowed.

![](//avc.qcloud.com/wiki2.0/im/imgs/20151015064428_35733.png)


### Add SMS Signature

Open the App list, and then click **App Configuration** for the relevant App.
![](//avc.qcloud.com/wiki2.0/im/imgs/20151120085729_37616.png)
Click **Edit** next to **Account System Integration**.
![](//avc.qcloud.com/wiki2.0/im/imgs/20151120085752_94804.png)
Enter the SMS signature in the text box next to the **SMS Signature Verification** in the required format.
![](//avc.qcloud.com/wiki2.0/im/imgs/20151120085817_46612.png)


### Upload Public Key

Open the App list, and then click **App Configuration** for the relevant App.

![](//avc.qcloud.com/wiki2.0/im/imgs/20151120085014_25180.png)

Click **Edit** next to **Account System Integration**.

![](//avc.qcloud.com/wiki2.0/im/imgs/20151120085205_27562.png)

Then click **Upload Public Key**.

![](//avc.qcloud.com/wiki2.0/im/imgs/20151120085328_40453.png)


### Download Public Key

![](//avc.qcloud.com/wiki2.0/im/imgs/20151123112657_31405.png)

### Download UserSig

Open the App list, and then click **App Configuration** for the relevant App.

![](//mccdn.qcloud.com/static/img/374227b79b09ba5c157b43a7e8667971/image.png)

Click **Download** in the **Download User Credential** column of **Account System Integration** to obtain the UserSig generated by the system.
