Log in to Tencent Cloud Console, and click "Cloud Products" - "Cloud Automated Testing" to enter the automated testing task management page. Then, click on the "New Task" button in the upper-left corner.
![](//mccdn.qcloud.com/img5694f4a0bcc33.png)

Configure automated testing address: An automated testing address is generally the public network address or domain name for developers to provide services externally.
![](//mccdn.qcloud.com/img5694f51a8cda3.png)

Configure automated testing plan: Enter the name of automated testing task, alarm plan, and automated testing point settings. After the alarm is generated, developers can view the specific alarm information in "Automated Testing Alarm Data".
![](//mccdn.qcloud.com/img5694f55824336.png)

After confirming that the configuration information is correct, the creation is complete.
![](//mccdn.qcloud.com/img5694f5781cd72.png)
