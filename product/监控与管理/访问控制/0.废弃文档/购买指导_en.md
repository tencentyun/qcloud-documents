## 1. Overview

You don't need to activate the CAM service, but to apply for the registration of a Tencent Cloud account. This service is free of charge.

## 2. Method

To activate the CAM service, you need to apply for the registration of a Tencent Cloud account. For more information, please see [relevant document](https://cloud.tencent.com/document/product/378/8415).
