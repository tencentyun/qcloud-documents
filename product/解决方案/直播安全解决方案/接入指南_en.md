How to access Tencent Cloud LVB security solution:

![接入](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/2d73bd040cee3c2bd5bf38183340f1f2/image.png)

## Registering an Account
Register an account at [Tencent Cloud official website](https://cloud.tencent.com/);
If necessary, create a collaboration account for common management under **Account Center** -> **Project Management** -> **New Project**;

![项目管理](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/b7ab6fa1456036c90d50fac0bce063d1/image.png)

On new project page, create policies and project groups under **User and Permission**.

![用户与权限](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/a444f058229e0b71fe6ecf5e16ce20dc/image.png)

## Applying for Activation
Go to **Cloud Product** -> **Video Service** -> **LVB** on [Tencent Cloud official website](https://cloud.tencent.com/);

![直播](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/23cff8f499e1e889040845fe9946eefe/image.png)

Click **Apply for Activation** after entering the "Apply to activate VOD solution" page.

![申请开通](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/9071880b8ef79941182d9f04f6cea57c/image.png)

## Configuring Features
Configure screenshot porn detection feature as needed after activating the service permission.

## Registering and Logging in Porn Detection Platform
Register an account on [Porn Detection Management Platform]( http://jh.live.qcloud.com) and login.

## Management and Operations on Porn Detection Platform
At last, manage and operate videos on the porn detection platform.
