Tencent Cloud is currently available in a number of available regions: **East China (Shanghai) , South China (Guangzhou) and North China (Beijing)**;
1. Cloud products in the same region communicate with each other through private networks.
2. Cloud products in different regions cannot communicate with each other through private networks.
3. When purchasing cloud services, you're recommended to choose the region closest to your customers in order to minimize access latency and improve download speed.
