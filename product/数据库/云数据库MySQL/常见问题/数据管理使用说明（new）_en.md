## 1. Instructions for using Data Management Console (DMC)
1.1. On [Cloud Database Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cdb), click "Log In" next to the instance in the "Operation" column to go to the login page of database management console, as shown below:
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/e88ef23e20a2bb9870c23063a267fe0f/image.png)
1.2. Select the region and instance (default to the instance you just selected), enter the account ID and password, and then click "Log In" to enter the database management console, as shown below:
![](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/51a078f9117cc5ec1e9765ff727ac42b/image.png)
1.3. You can view and manage instance sessions and display the real-time performance information of instances on the DMC.

## 2. PMA (PhpMyAdmin) entry on DMC
1. After logging in to the DMC, you can return to the original PMA page by clicking "Back to PMA" on the top navigation bar without logging in again.
