You can register a Tencent Cloud account with email or you can log in to Tencent Cloud with third-party accounts, such as WeChat, WeChat Official Account, and QQ ID.

**Enterprise users** are recommended to register Tencent Cloud account with email or WeChat Official Account. **Users not in Mainland China** are recommended to register with email.


### 1. Register with Email

**Step 1: Enter the registration process**

Click "[Register to Win Gifts](https://cloud.tencent.com/register)" at the navigation or click "[Register](https://cloud.tencent.com/register)" at the bottom of the login page to enter the registration process.

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/583b467beb32817cafd46fe49eb5c05c/zhucerukou2.png)
![ ](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/1c8efeeb1d71317efcdd6e5b3e0159ba/image.png)

**Step 2: Enter the registration information**

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/8198242cc2bad3d2415fc7f8d7f57835/3.png)
Note: Password should be a combination of 6-20 characters comprised of at least two of the following types: letters, numbers, special characters.

**Step 3: Activate the email**

![Activate Email](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/8d2cf2b8725b4e2d7a00da54332995e4/image.png)

**Step 4: Enter the industry information**

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/b3508c2496948d24dedb70c7e84ae485/hangye.png)

**Notifications of successful registration**

### 2. Register with WeChat

 **Step 1: Scan the QR code**
 
Click "WeChat Login" on the login page

![WeChat Login](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/5a08fe54336c7bcbbdb285f7f9e5d1ae/weixindenglu.png)

Open Scan QR Code on your Wechat client, and scan the QR code shown on a new page of your computer

![WeChat QR Code](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/83f81884267b00b7828d4ad8cca19953/weixin2.png)


 **Step 2: Authorize**
 
Click Authorize on the Wechat client

**Step 3: Register**` (required for first login)`

Enter the phone number -> email -> select the industry -> click Complete the Registration
> Note: Each mobile number can be associated with up to 5 Tencent Cloud accounts

![Complete Information](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/171940de3430748142311d5b12585393/image.png)

 **Step 4: Log in**
 
Log in successfully. Start your Tencent Cloud journey!

### 3. Register with Wechat Official Account

Only users with WeChat Official Accounts are allowed to register and log in to Tencent Cloud conveniently.

 **Step 1: Scan the QR code**
 
Click "WeChat Official Account" to log in on the login page

![Log in with WeChat Official Account](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/eebf89f4eadeb7b8fb3d4869dffef1ae/weixingongzhonghao.png)


Open Scan QR Code on your WeChat client bound to WeChat Official Account

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/db96dab81ea00394de4ef2c89bbff5f5/3.png)

Scan the QR code shown on a new page

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/bac6b84a00a32a3ee0c287fac01785ee/2.png)

 **Step 2: Authorize**
 
Select the WeChat Official Account to log in to Tencent Cloud

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/9d7d8182b7facd0df6fd28cd1597ce58/4.png)

> Note: Be sure to select the service permission of your account. Otherwise it will lead to failed login.

Click Authorize

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/f922081ddb134fe4858b8ada75ed50f3/5.png)

Successful authorization

**Step 3 Register**` (required for first login)`

Enter the phone number -> select the industry -> click Complete the Registration

> Note: Each mobile number can be associated with up to 5 Tencent Cloud accounts
![Complete Information](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/171940de3430748142311d5b12585393/image.png)

After the registration is completed, you will return to the page you visited before registration/login.

 **Step 4: Log in**
 
Log in successfully. Start your Tencent Cloud journey!

### 4. Register with QQ

Click [Login](https://cloud.tencent.com/login), enter your QQ number and password, and click Log in

![Log in with QQ](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/4d6fa1d1f7681721e3d0bf91dc74989d/dengluqq.png)

After the verification is passed, enter the phone number, verification code, select your own industry, and finally click Complete the Registration.

![Complete Information](//mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/171940de3430748142311d5b12585393/image.png)
