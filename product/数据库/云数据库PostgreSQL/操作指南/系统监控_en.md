**Metrics Supporting Alarm**
Currently, alarm is available only for the key performance metrics. For other metrics, alarm is not supported in Cloud Monitor.

In the instance management list, click the "Manage" button for the instance on the right side of the page.

![](//mccdn.qcloud.com/static/img/aa3c3ac8e0869b2d3188c204e967398f/image.png)
Select the "System Monitor" tab to perform real-time monitoring of the relevant data.
![](//mccdn.qcloud.com/static/img/7a67efab3db8f2594ad216397ee6f40c/image.png)
