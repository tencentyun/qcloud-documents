### 	Instance Expansion
1. Click the "Expand" button in the instance list page of the console, select the target capacity you wish to expand to and click "OK"
![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/cd2e09eb1d08b80b8c1acfe62040722e/kuoron.png)

Note:
1. When expanding master/slave version, if the current machine does not have enough capacity for the expansion, migration operation will be commenced. This will not affect business access.
2. The maximum capacity for master/slave version is 60 GB, thus you cannot expand beyond this capacity.
