1. If your account balance is insufficient, BWP will continue to be available for use and billed for another 2 hours after the balance becomes 0.
2. If your account is not topped up for a positive balance after 2 hours, BWP will be automatically stopped and no more charges will be incurred.
3. If your account is not topped up for a positive balance within 24 hours after BWP goes out of service, BWP will remain unavailable until your account balance is above zero.
4. If your account balance is below zero for more than 24 hours after BWP goes out of service, BWP will be reclaimed immediately.
5. When reclaiming BWP, we will notify the creator of your Tencent Cloud account and all collaborators via email and SMS.
