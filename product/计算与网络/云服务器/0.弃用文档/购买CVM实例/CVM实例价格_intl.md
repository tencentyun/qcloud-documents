## 1.	Postpaid
This section describes the pricing rules for postpaid CVMs. 

From November 13, 2017, postpaid CVMs did not support tiered pricing.  Newly purchased and existing CVM instances shall refer to the new pricing standards. Under the new pricing program, you can save more money for longer usage.
