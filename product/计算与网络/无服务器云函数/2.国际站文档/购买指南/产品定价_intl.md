| Billing Item | Unit | Price |
|---|---|---|
| Resource usage | GBs | 0.0000167 USD/GBs |
| Number of calls | 1 million calls | 0.15 USD/million calls |
| Public network outbound traffic | GB | The price per GB varies depending on each region |
