## 1. What is Direct Connect? What is it used for?
Tencent Cloud Direct Connect is a private network interconnection service. It can be used to connect Tencent Cloud VPC with your local data center, to deploy a flexible and reliable hybrid cloud architecture for providing IT services.
## 2. What are the benefits of Direct Connect?
Direct Connect is used for private network connection, with its bandwidth exclusively used, and financial-level network interconnect solutions can be provided through dual-line redundancy of entire network.
## 3. What areas are Direct Connect available for?
Currently, Direct Connect is available for most of the mainland region. For the specific construction conditions, please check offline with our Direct Connect managers. (Currently, Direct Connect service is only limited to mainland China. For Direct Connect requirements outside mainland China, such as Hong Kong, North America etc., please contact us offline).
## 4. How do I get started with Direct Connect?
You can apply for your first Direct Connect through the Direct Connect console. You will be prompted to enter the necessary information and leave valid contacts. Our Direct Connect manager will then contact you offline, to guide you through the construction of a Direct Connect.
## 5. How long does it take for Direct Connect?
Time for Direct Connect depends on the resource preparation for your local data center. In the case of adequate resources, for Direct Connect of Telecom Shanghai or Shenzhen and China Unicom, it can be setup within 16 business days for city-wide ones and 25 business days for cross-city ones. For Direct Connect in other cities or of other ISP, please check with our Direct Connect managers offline for feasible setup time.
