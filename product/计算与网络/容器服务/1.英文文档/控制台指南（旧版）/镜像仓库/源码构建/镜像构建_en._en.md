### Image Building Page
1. First, open the image registry list.
![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/e6d36fe97ace987fb412cbe53f372996/image.png)

2. Click an image registry to go to the details page, on which you can view the building rules of the image registry and the building history.

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/301aaeab159aac7faa535bbeb65e63e5/image.png)

### Viewing Image Building History
Click the "Building Log" button to view the log of building records.
![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/8cbc601264349a5e75028ddf487c4d07/image.png)

### Build Now
You can manually build an image registry once right now.
