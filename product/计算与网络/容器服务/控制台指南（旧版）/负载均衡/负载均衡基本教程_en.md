## Creating Load Balancer

### TCP/UDP
The public network-based or private network-based Layer-4 load balancer is automatically created according to the access type when the service is created.
### HTTP/HTTPS
On the load balancer page, click to create a load balancer and enter the basic information.

## Deleting Load Balancer
### TCP/UDP
The load balancer is automatically deleted when the service is deleted.
### HTTP/HTTPS
On the HTTP/HTTPS load balancer list page, click to delete the load balancer.
