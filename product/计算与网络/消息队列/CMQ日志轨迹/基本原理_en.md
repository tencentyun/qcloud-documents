**Definition:** Log trace refers to the complete link information such as when and where the producer sends the message to the consumer and the consumer consumes the message.

**Principle:** In CMQ system, a complete link of a message includes a producer, a CMQ server and a consumer. They will add some information in the trace link when processing the message. By collecting the information, you can know the status of the message, so as to provide strong data support for troubleshooting the problems in the production environment.
