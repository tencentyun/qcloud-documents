## 1. Shut down instances on CDH console

> 1. Log in to [CDH Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cvm/cdh).
> 2. Click the CDH ID/name to enter CDH details page.
> 3. Click "CVM List".
> 4. Select the dedicated CVM and shut down it.



## 2. Shut down instances through CVM Console/APIs

Dedicated CVM is shut down in the same way as ordinary CVM.

[See documents about shutting down instances>](https://cloud.tencent.com/doc/product/213/4929)
