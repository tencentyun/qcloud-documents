To use GCC instances better, **read the following notes thoroughly and keep them in mind**:
#### 1. Data Backup
GCC instances are designed to provide powerful computing capacities. They are mounted with local SSD disks by default. Make sure to back up data periodically to ensure data safety and prevent data loss in extreme circumstances.
To ensure better data safety, you can also purchase and mount elastic cloud disks separately.
#### 2. Account Renewal
You're notified 7 days before the expiration date for your GCC instances. In case of expiration, a GCC instance is shut down, disconnected and placed in the Recycle Bin. Be sure to renew in time or back up your data before expiration date.
#### 3. Connect External Devices
For now, you cannot use external hardware devices (such as hardware protection dongles, USB disks, external disks, U-keys for banks) directly on GCC instances.
#### 4. Upgrade Configuration
For now, GCC instance configuration cannot be adjusted after purchase.
#### 5. Forbidden Operations
  -  **Selling or renting the traffic of your GCC instances to third parties** is strictly prohibited. The highest penalty will be termination, lockup and clear-up of your instances.
  -  It is not allowed to use GCC instances to perform these activities against e-commerce websites such as Taobao.com: click farming (order, sales volume or advertisement), making **bogus website transactions**.
