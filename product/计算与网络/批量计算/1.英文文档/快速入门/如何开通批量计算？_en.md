## 1. Access Application Page
Currently, Batch is under internal trial. You can access [Apply for Batch](https://cloud.tencent.com/act/apply/Batch) page to submit an application.

## 2. Fill in Application

![Fill in Application](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/bdc74dd345c694298c3263e5c68b6b1f/image.png)
 
You must **completely** fill in the **actual** information. How quickly your application is handled and whether the application is approved depend on the completeness and accuracy of the information.

## 3. Wait for Approval Result
After the application is submitted, our staff will contact you and review your application. The approval result is sent to you via SMS.
