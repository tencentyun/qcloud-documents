## Creating a Job

For more information on jobs, please see "Job" in the [Glossary](https://cloud.tencent.com/document/product/599/10396). You can create a job in the [BatchCompute Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/batch/task).

1. Log in to the [BatchCompute Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/batch/task). If you have not activated the BatchCompute service, activate it according to the prompts on the BatchCompute Console page.

2. Click the **Job** option in the left navigation bar, select the target region, and then click the **New** button.
![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/7bc845062d2b91aac30f548511ab183c/image.jpg)

3. Configure the basic information for the job.
![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/adfad5bef466330a4f5583a84531f4af/image.jpg)

4. Select the task template on the left side of **Task Flow**, drag the task to the right canvas, and then drag the anchor to create a connection.
 ![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/8c2f1e4e4121a5beaf874425c47a243f/image.jpg)

5. Open **Task Details** on the right side of **Task Flow**. Confirm the information, and then click the **OK** button.
   + In the job task flow, each task is customized based on the task template.
   + Open the **Task Information** option on the right side and select a task to edit its configurations. The editing will not change the task template.
   ![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/7e8faba3818f7ff2ada687ed7602be2e/image.jpg)
   
## Terminating a Job
You can terminate a job in certain conditions. For more information on terminating a job, please see [Terminate Task Instances](https://cloud.tencent.com/document/product/599/12688).

## Deleting a Job
When a job is in the SUCCEED or FAILED status, you can delete it from the job list.
![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/2abcf3d06f446395c8bafa0c955abd2f/image.jpg)
