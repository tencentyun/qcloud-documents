1) Log in to [Tencent Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/), click "Virtual Private Cloud" on the top bar, and select "Security" - "Network ACL" tabs on the left.

2. Click the ID of the network ACL to be modified to enter the network ACL details page.

3. Click the "Basic Info" tab, and in the Associated Subnets list, click the "Unbind" button at the end of the subnet entry to be unbound; or check all the subnets to be unbound, and click "Batch unbind" to unbind the subnets from the network ACL.
