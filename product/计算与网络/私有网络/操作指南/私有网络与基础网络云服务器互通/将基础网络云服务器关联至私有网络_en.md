1) Log in to [CVM Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/), click "Virtual Private Cloud" in the navigation bar, then click a VPC ID in the VPC list in the VPC console to enter the details page of this VPC.

2) Click "Classiclink" tab, and click "Bind CVM" button.

3) In the pop-up window, check the CVMs in the basic network to be associated with the VPC, and click "OK" to complete the operation. The association takes effect immediately after your confirmation.
