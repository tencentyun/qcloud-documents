Charges for BM LB include two parts: LB instance fee and the fee for bandwidth/traffic generated during the access to the Internet.

### LB Instance Fee
The charge for using public network-based LB instance is 1 CNY/day. Private network-based LB instance is free of charge.

### Bandwidth/Traffic Fee
- Bill by Internet traffic: 0.80 CNY/GB (billed by total Internet traffic generated by all listeners).
