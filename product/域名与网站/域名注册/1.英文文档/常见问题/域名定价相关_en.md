### What's the Price for Domain Names?
We have different prices for purchasing domain names, transferring domain names and renewing domain names. See the Tencent Cloud official website for the latest prices. Click to view [List of Domain Name Prices](https://buy.cloud.tencent.com/domain?price=1).

### Is There a Special Offer if I Purchase Domain Name for More Years?
For now, Tencent Cloud does not provide special offer for the purchase of domain name for more years, but there is special offer for the purchase of domain name in the first year.

### Is There a Special Offer if I Register Domain Names in Batch?
We can provide a special offer according to the number of domain names you registered. You can [Submit Ticket](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/workorder/category/create?level1_id=16&level2_id=17&level1_name=%E5%85%B6%E4%BB%96%E6%9C%8D%E5%8A%A1&level2_name=%E5%9F%9F%E5%90%8D) to contact our salesperson to inquire about detailed prices.

### Will Domain Name Prices Change in the Future?
The prices of domain names (including prices for purchasing, renewal and transfer) may be adjusted according to market conditions. Users should follow the prices published on the official website to purchase domain names.
