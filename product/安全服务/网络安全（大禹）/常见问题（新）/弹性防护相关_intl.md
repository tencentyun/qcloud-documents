## Product Content
### What is the defense capability of Dayu high defense product?
It supports the elastic defense of 310 Gbps. Nanjing Telecom can support a defense of 600 Gbps.
### What will happen if the attack exceeds the set elastic defense value?
When the attack exceeds the defense value, your IP will be blocked.
## Product Implementation
### Can the defense against CC attacks be improved after the elastic defense is enabled?
The defense against CC attacks can be improved.
### If the attack is blocked, can it be unblocked after the elastic defense is enabled?
After the attack is blocked, it cannot be unblocked by enabling the elastic defense.
