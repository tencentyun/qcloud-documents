Tencent Cloud Dayu High Defense IP pushes an alarm message to you when your high defense IP is attacked, the attack ends, or the IP is blocked/unblocked.
Next, we show you how to configure a sub-account to receive the alarm messages of the main account.
1. Log in to your Tencent Cloud account, and go to the [Message Center](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/message/detail/45743360), or click the Message button in the upper right corner of the [User Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/dayu/bgpip), and then click **Enter Message Center** at the bottom of the popped-up page.
 ![1](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/ee10a3410b804b7c1e3f2ef4efdfe7a4.png)
2. Click **Message Subscription** in the left directory to enter the message list.
 ![2](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/8949571b0a2ea24d59fe229784795a15.png)
3. In the message list, find "Security Notification", and click **Edit** on the right to enter the editing page.
 ![3](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/df41adea8b860efc4009ecf59767d2cf.png)
4. Select the recipient and the receiving method, and click **OK**.
 ![4](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/2098588e7520bc1b4d29bc910cc1729d.png)
