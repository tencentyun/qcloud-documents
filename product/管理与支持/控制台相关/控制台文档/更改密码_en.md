The account owner can change the password of his/her Tencent Cloud account in [Tencent Cloud Console](https://cloud.tencent.com/login?s_url=https%3A%2F%2Fconsole.cloud.tencent.com%2F).
 1. On the right of the top navigation bar, select your account name.
![Account](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/df25df820c51471d79b8142c847a1bc9/image.jpg)
 2. Select **Security Settings** and then select **Change Password**. For your account security, you need to perform mobile verification when changing the password, as shown below:
 ![Verification via mobile](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/6fd7f1fafe19cd178b32bf657ca5cde2/image.jpg)
 3. Enter your current password once, and then enter your new password twice.
   >Note: The password should be a combination of 6-20 characters comprised of at least two of the following types: letters, numbers, and special characters.
   
 4. After you complete the password sheet, select **OK** to save the changed password.
