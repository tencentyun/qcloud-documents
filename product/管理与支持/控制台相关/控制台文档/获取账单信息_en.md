You can get your bill information from Tencent Cloud Console.
1. In the right box of the top navigation bar, click **Billing Center** to query the bill information.
![New fees](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/9b957c7b2d884f2f7db6edf49d9e1af8/image.jpg)
2. Select **My Orders** to view your bill details.
3. Use **Fund Management** and **Bill Management** to find the summary and details of monthly expenses, as shown below:
![Billing Center](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/e0bdf46a38aaf031adce16f11a14f661/image.jpg)
