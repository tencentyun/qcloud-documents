You can sign up for a Tencent Cloud account in various channels. This document describes how to sign up by email. For more information on other sign-up methods, please see [Sign up for Tencent Cloud](https://cloud.tencent.com/document/product/378/9603).
### 1. Proceed with the sign-up process
Log in to the official website of Tencent Cloud at [https://cloud.tencent.com](https://cloud.tencent.com/), and then click the **Sign Up** button on the right side of the top navigation bar or the **[Sign Up Now](https://cloud.tencent.com/register?s_url=https%3A%2F%2Fcloud.tencent.com%2F%3FfromSource%3Dgwzcw.184926.184926.184926%26gclid%3DEAIaIQobChMI2Yb69OGQ2QIVzxOPCh3RKwUiEAAYASAAEgIthPD_BwE)** button at the bottom of the dialog box on the login page to proceed with the sign-up process.
![New 1](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/ae6cef5c215bc6d1099138026ddd7dbc/image.jpg)

### 2. Enter the sign-up information
Enter your basic information and bind with your mobile as prompted. After that, select Agree to [Tencent Cloud Terms of Service](https://cloud.tencent.com/document/product/301/1967) and [Tencent Cloud Privacy Statement](https://cloud.tencent.com/document/product/301/11470), and then click **Agree to the Terms and Submit**, as shown below:
![Sign-up](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/8ea760d93740b4f84848688c75b3551e/image.jpg)
### 3. Log in to Tencent Cloud Console
After sign-up is completed, go back to the home page of [Tencent Cloud](https://cloud.tencent.com/). After that, click **[Console](https://cloud.tencent.com/login?s_url=https%3A%2F%2Fconsole.cloud.tencent.com%2F)** on the right side of the top navigation bar to enter the login page of Tencent Cloud's Management Console.
### 4. Enter the login Information
Enter the login information and click **Log In**, or scan the QR code with WeChat, or use QQ quick login, to log in to Tencent Cloud Console. When the page is successfully redirected, **Welcome back, XXX** will be displayed on the right corner of the top navigation bar, indicating that you have logged in successfully:
![Successful](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/adf510c36b8a412d336368a33451d98a/image.jpg)
