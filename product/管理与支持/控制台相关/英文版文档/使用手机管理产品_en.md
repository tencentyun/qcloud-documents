Tencent Cloud allows easy management and use of cloud products on your mobile.
1. On the Console overview page, move the mouse cursor to the right side of the page, and then a QR code will pop up in the left, as shown below:
2. When the QR code of "Tencent Cloud Assistant" appears, open your mobile WeChat to scan this QR code, follow it, and link it with your Tencent Cloud account. After that, the information on cloud products or services can be monitored in real time and managed dynamically.
