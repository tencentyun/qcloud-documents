Most Tencent Cloud products have separate product consoles (such as CVM console and COS console) in which you can renew, configure, or delete them. On the [Tencent Cloud Console](https://cloud.tencent.com/login?s_url=https%3A%2F%2Fconsole.cloud.tencent.com%2F), you can enter a specific product console using any of the following methods.
**Method 1:**
In the left box in the top navigation bar, select **Cloud Products** to open the complete list of products and services, and then select the desired service.
![Select product](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/cf4c9764702ad9699761bc40f2a03bc4/image.jpg)
**Method 2:**
In the right search box in the top navigation bar, enter the name of the cloud product or service whose console you need to access, and then select the desired service from the list of search results.
![Search bar 3](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/a4921681c590a0fb31e08b580d9d937f/image.jpg)
**Method 3:**
If you have recently used a product console, select the recently accessed product or service in the right log bar in the top navigation bar.
![Recent logs](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/bbe9679a869c0f27f2efcad002153ec9/image.jpg)
