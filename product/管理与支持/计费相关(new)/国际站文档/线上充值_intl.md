Users need to top up their account to purchase Tencent Cloud services if they have negative balance in their account. To avoid service interruptions caused by arrears, It is suggested that users top up their account before using any cloud services. Please note that most Tencent Cloud services are billed by hour, therefore account arrears may cause service suspension.

To top up their account, the users can choose to pay with PayPal or Bank Transfers.

For data security, only creator and collaborator with authorized finance permission are allowed to enter the "Top-up" page.

## Top-up Process

### Enter the "Top-up" page

After logging in to Tencent Cloud, go to "**Console**" and click "**Top-up Now**", or go to "**Billing Center**" and click "**Top-up Now**" to enter the "**Top-up**" page.

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/7c8c0638e0c66b37e2c5b9aa68ba2972/image.png)

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/ac79830a28c133bb12422e8c39073fd4/image.png)

On the Top-up page, choose "**PayPal**" (recommended) or "**Bank Transfer Top-Up**" to top up account.

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/4fa6cf2137e5dbd0cd2b22cd1c1e68ee/image.png)


## Top-up Methods

### PayPal

1.On the PayPal page, manually enter a top-up amount (an integer between 1 and 9999999), or use last top-up amount, and then click "**Pay Now**"

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/ef94dad415150f4526bfb3c45471e4ec/image.png)

2.Click "**Check out**" on the payment page.

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/e1445bb2b79da3fc590bea7f1f47ffec/image.png)

3.Log in to PayPal account to proceed the payment.

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/23e6edfccb963d05bacb3945310068ae/image.png)

4.Once the payment is completed, a notice page will be shown as below. You can click "Back To Home" to go back to the Top-Up page. Click "Topped up successfully". Please note that it may take several seconds to update your account balance after the transaction is completed.

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/8315c54cfd14d5683b51025589a20ebd/image.png)

![](https://mc.qcloudimg.com/static/img/73f3b6cb708d93df373d636b159c9a8f/image.png)

5.For any question, you can submit a ticket in your console to contact us.

6.As payments via PayPal incurs charges, Tencent Cloud will bear all the charges.

### Bank Transfer

1.If you choose Bank Transfer, you directly transfer money to our bank account via online banking, check, wire transfer or bank deposit at the counter. You can check out our account information on the Bank Transfer page. Please include your Tencent Cloud account ID in remarks when you make the bank payment.

It usually takes 3 work days to update bank transfer amount into your account, given that you have submitted accurate payment information. However, cross-border transactions may take more time. If you have any question, you can submit a ticket in the console.

4.Please note that bank transfer may incur bank charges. As we cannot retrieve bank data, all bank charges will be borne by users.
